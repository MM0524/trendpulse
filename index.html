About TrendPulse

TrendPulse is a crowdsourced platform to predict and vote on emerging trends in technology, culture, and beyond.

How it works:

1. Submit: Find something you think is the next big thing? Submit it with a source link.

2. Vote: Browse the trends and cast your vote on the ones you believe in. You get 3 votes per day!

3. Discover: See what the community thinks is on the rise through our real-time leaderboard.
